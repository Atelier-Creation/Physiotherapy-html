Fertility Rehab

At Aura Physiotherapy and Rehab Centre, our Fertility Rehabilitation program is thoughtfully designed to support women in enhancing reproductive health, managing hormonal conditions, and preparing the body for conception and post-surgical recovery

Using a holistic, evidence-based approach that blends physiotherapy, pelvic health expertise, and stress management, our goal is to help restore balance — physically, hormonally, and emotionally.

Pelvic Pain & Dysfunction

Chronic pelvic pain can interfere with daily life, intimacy, and fertility. We identify and address the root cause of discomfort through pelvic floor therapy, muscle release techniques, posture correction, and relaxation therapy. Our approach helps relieve tension, improve blood flow, and reduce discomfort around the lower abdomen and pelvis.

Scar Tissue & Adhesions

After surgeries such as C-section, laparoscopy, or abdominal procedures, scar tissue can restrict organ mobility and affect fertility. Our scar mobilization therapy involves gentle manual techniques to break down adhesions, restore tissue mobility, and improve pelvic function for a healthier reproductive environment.

PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome)

PCOS often leads to hormonal imbalances, weight gain, and menstrual irregularities. At Aura, we support PCOS management with guided exercise programs, core strengthening, stress-relieving routines, and posture correction — all of which help balance hormones, manage insulin resistance, and improve overall health.

Stress & Anxiety

Mental health plays a critical role in fertility. Chronic stress can disrupt hormonal balance and menstrual cycles. Our relaxation-focused physiotherapy includes breathing techniques, guided movement, and mindfulness-based routines to reduce cortisol levels and support emotional well-being throughout your fertility journey.

Pre & Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Whether you’ve undergone a hysteroscopy, laparoscopy, myomectomy, or other reproductive surgery, physiotherapy plays a vital role in your recovery. We help manage pain, reduce swelling, improve abdominal mobility, and strengthen core muscles to prepare the body for healing and future pregnancy.

Urinary Incontinence

Urinary leakage or urgency is often due to pelvic floor dysfunction, especially post-partum or due to hormonal changes. Our pelvic floor rehabilitation focuses on strengthening the internal muscles, retraining the bladder, and improving pelvic stability — promoting confidence and control

Back Pain

Many women dealing with fertility issues also experience chronic lower back pain due to poor posture, weak pelvic muscles, or hormonal fluctuations. We offer spinal alignment therapy, lumbar strengthening, and mobility exercises to relieve pain and improve posture, especially during the fertility process.

General Well-being

A healthy body supports a healthy reproductive system. Our sessions include customized stretching, posture improvement, breathing practices, and light cardio routines to help you feel energized, reduce inflammation, and keep your body in optimal condition for fertility and hormonal balance.
