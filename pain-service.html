Pain Management

At Aura Physiotherapy and Rehab Centre, our Pain Management program is designed to provide long-term relief from a wide range of acute and chronic pain conditions. Whether your discomfort is caused by injury, posture, neurological issues, or autoimmune disorders — our evidence-based, non-invasive therapies focus on restoring comfort, mobility, and quality of life.

Our approach includes advanced physiotherapy, posture correction, therapeutic exercises, manual therapy, and modalities like ultrasound, TENS, and dry needling — tailored to your pain type and root cause.

Acute & Chronic Pain

Whether it’s sudden (acute) or long-standing (chronic), pain affects your body and mental well-being. At Aura, we conduct a thorough assessment to understand the source of your pain and develop a personalized treatment plan to relieve symptoms and prevent recurrence. Our focus is on long-term recovery, not just short-term relief.

Low Back Pain

Low back pain is one of the most common complaints among adults, often caused by poor posture, slipped discs, or sedentary lifestyles. Our treatments include spinal mobilization, core strengthening, ergonomic correction, and tailored movement therapies that restore lumbar stability and function.

Neck Pain

Neck pain may stem from prolonged screen time, bad posture, or muscle tension. Our neck rehabilitation program includes cervical traction, deep neck muscle strengthening, myofascial release, and electrotherapy — aimed at reducing stiffness, radiating pain, and restoring neck mobility.

Joint Pain

Whether due to arthritis, injury, or overuse, joint pain can restrict movement and independence. We offer joint mobilization, hydrotherapy exercises (if available), strengthening routines, and anti-inflammatory physiotherapy to relieve pain in knees, hips, ankles, and other joints.

Shoulder Pain

Common causes include frozen shoulder, rotator cuff injuries, and tendonitis. Our shoulder rehab combines manual therapy, posture correction, stretching routines, and strengthening of the scapular and rotator cuff muscles — all essential for restoring pain-free range of motion.

Musculoskeletal Pain

Musculoskeletal pain involves bones, muscles, tendons, ligaments, and joints. This could result from trauma, inflammation, repetitive strain, or systemic conditions. At Aura, we use soft tissue therapy, dry needling, kinesiology taping, and customized exercise protocols to treat:

  • Arthritis (inflammatory joint disorders)
  • Muscle strains and sprains
  • Tendonitis and bursitis
  • Bone-related stress and stiffness

Neuropathic Pain

This type of pain is caused by nerve damage or irritation — often felt as burning, tingling, or shooting pain. Our physiotherapy focuses on nerve mobilization, neurodynamic exercises, and pain modulation techniques to improve nerve function and reduce discomfort.

Autoimmune Disease-related Pain

Conditions like Fibromyalgia, Lupus, and Crohn’s Disease can trigger widespread inflammation, fatigue, and pain. Our treatment is gentle yet targeted — focused on energy conservation, pain management, postural support, and body reconditioning. We work to reduce flare-ups while enhancing mobility and resilience.

Headaches & Migraines

Tension-type headaches and cervicogenic migraines are often linked to poor posture, upper spine tension, or muscular imbalance. We offer cranio-cervical therapy, myofascial release, relaxation training, and postural correction to reduce headache frequency and intensity without medication dependency.
