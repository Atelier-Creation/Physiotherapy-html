How it work

We Proudly Give Quality Treatment

Book appointment with our expert physiotherapists through our online platform or by calling our clinic.

Chronic back or neck pain can limit your lifestyle. At Aura Physiotherapy, we offer personalized treatments using spinal mobilization, posture correction, and muscle release techniques. Our goal is to reduce pain, improve flexibility, and help you regain comfort and mobility—perfect for sedentary workers, seniors, or injury-related tension.

Aura’s post-surgical rehab helps you heal better and faster after joint, spine, or orthopedic surgeries. Our physiotherapists guide you through recovery exercises, pain management, and mobility training. We focus on strengthening, balance, and long-term function—ensuring a safe, smooth return to daily life with reduced complications.

Recover quickly and safely from sprains, tears, and overuse injuries with Aura’s expert sports physiotherapy. We combine hands-on therapy, strength-building, and prevention strategies tailored to your sport and body. Whether you're a professional or a fitness enthusiast, we’ll help you return to peak performance with confidence.
